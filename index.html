Bmw X5M

This is a BMW X5M. It was produced in 2009 and is still being produced. It is now updated, modern and fast. Its price in America is ~$130,000. In Uzbekistan it is ~$170,000-200,000. It has 617 horsepower in stock condition and accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in 3.7 seconds. Its engine is equipped with a 4.4 liter V8 turbo.

width="100"

Bmw m4 competition competition xdrive coupe

This is the BMW M4 Competition xDrive. It was launched in July 2021 and is still in production. It is now updated, fast and modern. Its price in America is $90,600. In Uzbekistan it costs around $150,000-170,000. It has 523 horsepower in stock condition, and can accelerate from 0-100 km/h in 3.4 seconds (even faster if tuned). It is equipped with a 3-liter V8 twin-turbo engine.
